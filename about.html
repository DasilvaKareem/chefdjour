Our Story

Chef Du Jour's

Ayanna K. Johnson

Chef de cuisine

Chef Ayanna Kerese's passion for food blossomed from an early age. Hailing from Jamaica, her taste buds were tantalized by a diverse array of cultural influences and flavors. Whether it was the amalgamation of African, Indian, Chinese, or other culinary traditions, Ayanna was captivated by the fusion of tastes that defined Jamaican cuisine. Growing up in a military environment, she had the opportunity to savor dishes from around the world, lovingly prepared by her mother and the mothers of her friends.

Our approach

ALWAYS FRESH INGREDIENTS

At Chef DuJour, our commitment to quality starts with our selection of ingredients. We pride ourselves on sourcing only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients for our dishes. We have established strong partnerships with small family farms and trusted food suppliers who share our passion for delivering excellence.

By working closely with these suppliers, we can ensure that our ingredients are sustainably sourced, free from additives or preservatives, and of the utmost freshness. We prioritize supporting local farmers and promoting a farm-to-table approach, as we believe it not only enhances the flavor of our dishes but also contributes to the overall well-being of our community and environment.

Our diverse menu is designed to cater to a wide range of tastes and preferences. Whether you're a seafood lover, a steak enthusiast, a fan of natural soups, a pasta connoisseur, a salad aficionado, or someone with a sweet tooth craving the finest desserts, Chef DuJour has something to tantalize your taste buds.
